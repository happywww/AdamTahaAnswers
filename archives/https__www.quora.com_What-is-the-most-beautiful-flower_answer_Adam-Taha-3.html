Originally Answered: Which is the most beautiful flower ?

Impatiens bequaertii or known as the Dancing Girls.

It’s a rare species from the rainforests of east Africa. The petals resemble a little girl in a skirt with outstretched arms. It’s best when they are high up than on the ground because you can see the petite full shape of the dancing girls.
