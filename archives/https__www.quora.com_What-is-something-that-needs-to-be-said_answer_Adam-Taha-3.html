“What you don’t grow through, you will go through again and again.”

You are going through a challenge.

Maybe it’s fear of approaching women. Maybe it’s fear of approaching men. Maybe getting a job. Maybe it’s finishing a book. Maybe it’s money but your habits just not cutting it and your lifestyle is a mess.

Maybe you’re girlfriend or boyfriend left you and it’s got you into a dark place and you’re continually ranting, blaming or waiting for them to change. So you are afraid to move on because you fear the unknown.

Maybe you didn’t do what you need to do because it means doing the not so easy tasks and you’re up shit creek right now because your unproductive habits has affected everything for you.

Whatever it is, think of this phase as growth as you remember these words I say time and time again..

“What you don’t grow through, you will go through again and again.”

From A to B is growth and you decide, no one else but you on how long you will stay in this part.

The problem is the blaming and the insecurities might be your reality at this moment in time or you can decide to create your own reality and make it bigger than your insecurities to take positive productive actions.

You can decide right now…

“Enough is enough. I’m going to grow through it.”

If you don’t grow through it, you will go through this pain over and over and over again. You will always feel helpless. You will always fear rejection so bad and you will stay stuck.

You will let situations, circumstances and whatever you want to blame to prevent you to grow through it and it be like a broken record, going round and round.

You will always continue to ask why nice guys don’t get girls, and why assholes and bad boys get the girls….and why women decide this and that. On and on it goes to stay put in that dark place.

Here’s the plan…

You’re goal is to get to B and when you do….you will get to C with such speed you will wonder why on earth you didn’t do this in the first place. The most difficult phase is from A-B.

Here’s why…

It starts when the person stops blaming someone or situations for whatever failures and figure out the solution…

How long do you want to be in phase A to B is up to you.

  • You want to continue blaming assholes?
  • You want to continue blaming bad boys?
  • You want to continue blaming your looks, your height, your lack of education, lack of money and women and other guys?
  • You want to continue doing what hasn’t worked for you?

You can do whatever you want but it is you who pays that price. You will go through it again and again, and again.

You can call someone an asshole, a bad boy and you can blame it on anything your heart desires to feel better for a while to stay in the comfort zone.

You can believe all the social norms you like and all the things most people tell you which hasn’t worked for you.

You stay there to face the same helpless situation, behaving as a victim, blaming or believing whatever you want to believe.

Or you start with a positive and stronger reality…

Attitude is not enough.

It’s s great start but it’s not enough.

Before investing in specialised knowledge and skills….you will need to overcome your old habits that kept you stuck

You will need to sort out your thinking.

You will need to sort out your negative self talk which is programming you consistently to play the game of a victim.

You will need to sort out your social awkwardiness when approaching girls or guys. You will need to work on yourself first to sort out your lack of confidence and your low self.

You will need to understand your self more than ever to ensure the foundation is rock solid first or everything else is a waste of time.

The enemy is not out there. It’s in here. In you.

The great obstactle has always been yourself.

You’re own psychology, your own script, that game you have in your mind is your true enemy.

It is social masks you wear to cover up your fears of the true story.

It is preventing you to succeed.

You have to figure what that script is. Know where it came from. How did you see yourself and life like this.

That script is reinforced by your own behavioiur to make your world predictable. You make it predictable by making sure the poor results happen by blaming others or anything outside yourself to cover up your own insecurities.

Why? It’s easier to blame. It makes you feel comfortable.

Face your demons or your script will tkeep you in that phase for a lot longer and maybe for the rest of your life.

When you have done that…it is not over…

But it’s an awesome start..

You now have to sort your life out to invest in the specialised knowledge and skills. No more cutting corners.

Notice how you are still in the growth period?

A to B can take you 6 months -2 years or much longer. Most men and women who are unwilling to sort their baggage out take 20 years because they wait too long and have been comfort but the pain is now too much.

Some people stay there and never really grow to their full potential.

It all depends you.

Avoid the cliche of comparing your failues or success on anyone. This is about you battling it out with yourself.

When you got the knowledge and skill, you now need to learn how to apply, one step at a time. You don’t move from one thing until you get an understanding about it and get good at it.

Then you move on to the next and next and next while repeating then process.

  • Learning has it’s own process.
  • You apply and that has it’s own process.
  • You measure and that has it’s own process
  • Improving has it’s own process.

Learn, apply, measure and mprove to make it work for you.

Now we are in business because when you apply without over thnking too much and just get it done, you reach B quicker.

The moment you reach to B, you keep going.

Just because you tasted some success, doesn’t mean to slow down. It’s not over yet. Don’t be too hasty to stop!

Put your peddal down.

This is the time to hammer it and keep going on creating a huge momentumn. You will get to C faster than the growth period.

If the growth period took you 2 years, it will take you months to get from B to C. That’s how fast it can be.

However, the biggest challenge for people is a person has to get grow through the pain to get to B first.

The longer you avoid doing so, the longer you keep going through the same frustrations over and over again.

That’s why..

“What you don’t grow through, you will go through again and again.”
