Vultures circle the sky as crows peck at her marble corpse
Corruption, greed, prejudice and favors is how it now works
Bloggers to mainstream and anonymous source
And everyone’s opinion is now the judge and supreme court

The ghost of founding fathers sit with her in deep remorse
Waiting to bring Lady Justice to life again so the judging is in the courts
But so many got trapped in the vacuum of the mainstream reports
Society got caught up in watching the modern witch hunt sport

Gone are the days when innocent until proven guilty is applied
Reason and law is pushed aside for the undignified
And that’s when the power of mainstream media is glorified
That became the day when the Lady of Justice died
