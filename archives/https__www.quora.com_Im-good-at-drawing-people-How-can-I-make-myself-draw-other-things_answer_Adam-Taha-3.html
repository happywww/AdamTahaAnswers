I'm good at drawing people. How can I make myself draw other things?
Adam Taha

Everything has form. So study form…shapes.

Best thing to do, is get lots of references and begin understanding their forms. Not just the shapes but also observe where the light source is coming from, form the shape with shadows after you do the lines.

My lines a bit messy at moment but I am trying to limit them.

Lots of practising form…shapes and when you do, you’ll be able to draw anything. So have a sketch book and draw the different shapes you see.

The video will help to give more information on why, what and how.
