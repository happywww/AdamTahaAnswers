When you were with someone who in retrospect you realized was wrong for you, what were the signs while you were with them?
Adam Taha
Artist and writer passionate about psychology of dating · Author has 5K answers and 94.5M answer views · 6y ·

The first is…

1. Self centred.

Much of conversation and interaction was about them. You find they never really take interest in you, your life, passion and most of all…you give more while they…give far less…

2. They don’t speak your emotional language while you work hard to learn theirs, and speak theirs. It becomes emotionally draining and you feel it’s gamey, you feel it’s testing and you feel it’s all attention seeking…

3. As time goes by you begin to see they are not who they say they are and there is a compatibility issue..

It begins to change you as you see the person isn’t going to be able to satisfy your needs, wants and pleasures.

Many red flags like these and more begin to appear especially how they are not as certain as you are of the relationship, and you see they got trust issues, and yet you didn’t.

So this can change you to get so turned off to walk away and move on.

Nothing is going to change no matter how you think love will conquer all. Love won’t fix lack of compatibility and a lot of the issues you see on here.

It’s best to move on.
