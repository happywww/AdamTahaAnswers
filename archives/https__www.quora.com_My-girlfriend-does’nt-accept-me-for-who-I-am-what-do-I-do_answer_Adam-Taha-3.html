There was this young boy who returned home and he was chased by a wolf. Seeing how he could not escape from the world, he turned round and said..

“I know, that I must be your prey but before I die I only ask one favour. That you will play me a tune so I can dance.”

The Wolf agreed to play a tune and as he was piping away the Kid danced.

But some hounds hearing the sound ran up and began chasing the Wolf. He turned to the kid and said…

“It is just what I deserve; for I, a Wolf, who am only a butcher, should not have turned to be a piper to….please…you.”

And with that he ran away as he was chased by the hounds to find himself facing no escape but death.

I have learnt early on in my young years to never change for anyone. It only brings a fate worse than you can imagine especially if you are trying to please someone else. It never ends well.

I have walked away from many women who try to change and fix me.

To get to the women I desire and who desire men like me, without trying to fix me takes a man to have self respect.

I had to approach many women and filter and screen out. I have boundaries. They knew the boundaries well.

She is testing your frame and she has probably tested your frame before and you let her. Step by step she will try more and more until when she has done all she desires, she will say to you she is not in love with you.

Give you all kinds of bullshit and then be with men like me, who she knows she cannot do a damn thing with her controlling ways. She’ll be happy too because she knows no matter what she does…she knows she can’t do the same to this man, like she did to other men.

I am not broken and never have been.

Nor are you.

You don’t need to change and be fixed.

People know from day one who they are dating and getting into, and yet, they just want to control, manipulate and change you. They are manipulative and controlling. They will ever be happy with who you are.

You know what to do.

You show her the door and for her to realise, her manipulative and controlling ways are over.
