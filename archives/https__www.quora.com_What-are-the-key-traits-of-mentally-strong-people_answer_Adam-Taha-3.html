There are many but I will only share two because if you can get these two sorted, then you will for sure, be able to sort all the rest.

Confidence and Mental Toughness.

It is not easy to do but as time you will grow to have a consistent state of frame of mind that is stronger than a massive crowd shouting at you.

It doesn’t mean you won’t fail and falter.

It means you are humble in that you embrace growth to keep moving forward. No one is invincible. We are human beings and so we will fail, fall to rise up due to these two.

In doing so you will grow in empathy in the struggle of others.

So much so many will trust you because you are consistent when you are in a time of excitement and when excitement runs out. You are consistent when you are infatuated and when you are not. You are consistent whether good times or hard times.

Women will desire you more in greater numbers and they will even wonder what is it you got that attracts them. They cannot figure it out. It’s strong, powerful, magnetic but they just cannot put it in words.

You are confident and comfortable in your own skin. You have mental toughness and consistent when you get trolls in life:

You are consistent when people come at you seeking knowledge, expertise, your time but you are willing to say no, no matter what because you choose wisely who to give your time and expertise to.

You’ll be glad you did as you see their true face and character stand out to say you was right to say no…

You have a purpose. You don’t do things to seek approval, validation and acceptance but you keep doing what you do. In doing so, you are calm, collected and the result is you begin to change lives even with silence…

I get many messages. I disabled the comment for many reasons but the number one reason I do so is this - for a healthy mind. That mind took a lot to nurture and therefore, no one is going to tell you or me, what they are entitled to.

They can shout, scream like little children for not getting what they want and create lots of questions why authors disable the comment. They can assume many things but let them. Your mind needs to be protected and let your ego die for you all care. You have nothing to prove but to do your work to the best you can.

I have forged these traits like a black smith pounding away with steel hammer to build within the furnace inside me.

Ripping every social norm, tradition and belief I can from societal conditioning to be the man I desire to become.

It is not easy.

I sometimes fail. I sometimes lose track. I sometimes falter but the confidence tells me it’s part of my growth. To get back up and keep moving forward.

It tells me I need to keep growing.

The mental toughness keeps me bouncing back up fast to keep moving forward.

Nothing we desire to last and build is ever easy but it will last us longer than what we can buy today and what illusions of this world so loves to promise.

It will stand strong against mediocrity and the noise that tries to distract you from your own purpose and vision in life.

Hold these in your hand like burning coal for it will feel like that but nothing you want so great comes easy and without some pain.

They are…

Confidence and Mental Toughness.

You will change lives with these two even in…silence.
