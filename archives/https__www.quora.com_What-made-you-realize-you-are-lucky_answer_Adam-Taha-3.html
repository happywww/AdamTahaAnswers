Many people do things and work in jobs, or follow a path that isn’t really theirs. Their life seem tedious, they are bored and some feel they have no direction. Many still ask how to find their passion.

Dictated by someone else’s belief, relion, culture, social norm rules, parents belief system, friends belief system and on and on it goes, to continue to wear many social masks that they don’t know who they are anymore.

People know what they want. People know what makes them feel alive. People also know what they don’t want but they see what they got to let go of, the comfort zone and it’s just scary for them.

The path of uncertainty needs courage.

On the other hand, there is another inside of them that has so many goals, dreams, adventures, hopes, fantaasies they lock inside of them, wishing they could live them in their lives. Secrets they’d never ever share to close friends.

But for me, I have always just did what made me feel alive.

When I look back my wealth won’t be mansions, cars, millions of dollars and smile at these. It be the lives of people who have changed, grow, found the courage and a way to be happy, who realised something, got their “Aha!” moment…and able to connect the dots to why, what and how to move forward in their lives.
