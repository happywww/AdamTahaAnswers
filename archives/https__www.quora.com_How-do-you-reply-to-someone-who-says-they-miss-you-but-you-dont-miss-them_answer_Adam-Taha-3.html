How do you reply to someone who says they miss you but you don't miss them?
Adam Taha
Songwriter, Creative Writing on dating and relationships · Author has 5K answers and 94.4M answer views · 8y ·

This happened last week...

Someone - Hey Adam, great to see you. I have missed you man.

Me - Great to see you too. (or you can just say thanks and talk about something else)

Me - So listen. I told the woman I met I can cook Pizza's and I found out her dad owns a Pizza Hut franchise

Someone - Dude you are so screwed!

Me - I know. I know. I went and bought this book on cooking Pizza's and you are going to help me.

Someone = WTF! No way man.

Me - You said you missed me right?

Someone - Erm, yes and?

Me - Well, see it as time to catch up

That's what we did...

And it came out delicious...
